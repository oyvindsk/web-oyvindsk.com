A few places you can find Golang remote jobs

April 2016 

                "Go is an open source programming language that makes it easy to build simple, reliable, and efficient software (golang.org)".
Go (or golang) is a simple, general purpose programming language that excels at writing things like backend services. It's the language used to make Docker, among many other things.

Here's a few places you can find remote friendly Go jobs and freelance projects.

Websites specifically for Golang

Golangprojects.com

http://www.golangprojects.com/golang-remote-jobs.html

Probably the largest Golang specific website for job listings. Also has developer profiles. Most jobs are full time. Remote jobs can sometimes be US-specific.

We Love Golang

http://www.welovegolang.com/jobs/search?q=remote

Good site. Searching for "remote" is not entierly reliable.

Websites specifically for remote jobs

RemoteOK

https://remoteok.io/remote-golang-jobs

Great site with reposts jobs from other places. Often clicking the "Apply for job" button will take you to the original website.

Remotely Awesome Jobs

https://www.remotelyawesomejobs.com/tags/golang?q=

Similar, fewer listings

FlexJobs.com

https://www.flexjobs.com/search?search=golang&location=

Remote / telecomute jobs site.

Golang Forums etc

Gophers Slack channel

https://gophers.slack.com/messages/golang-jobs/

The Gophers Slack is very active and has a separete "Jobs" channel.

The Golang forum

https://forum.golangbridge.org/c/jobs

The Golang-Jobs Google+ Community

https://plus.google.com/communities/116819843904802051830

Hacker News Monthly Postings

http://hnhiring.me/

Easy interface to search the news.ycombinator.com "Ask HN: Who is hiring?" posts. You can filter on words. More geeky than most job listing sites =)

General job listing sites

Stack Overflow Jobs

Remote jobs tagged with golang:
http://stackoverflow.com/jobs?searchTerm=[golang]&allowsremote=true

Remote jobs containing the word "golang":
https://stackoverflow.com/jobs?searchTerm=golang&allowsremote=true

Monster.com

I was supriesd to find a few hits for "golang" and "remote" on Monster.com:
http://jobs.monster.com/search/?q=golang&where=remote
